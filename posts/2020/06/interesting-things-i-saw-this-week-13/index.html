Interesting things I saw this week - 13

This week was the definition of dead. Here is the only thing worth sharing that I saw this week and the corresponding music recommendation.

  • [podcast] Private Suite Podcast : Vapor Memory Interview: This was an incredible find, I didn’t know there was a podcast dedicated to vaporwave until Vapor Memory uploaded this interview on his channel.

    Being the go-to channel to discover vaporwave on youtube, Vapor Memory is a very well known name in the vaporwave community, a name I would say as well known as some of the older and well established vaporwave artists.

    Listening to this interview felt incredible, the feeling of discovery when hearing for the first time the voice of someone you know only as their online persona is something like finding money on the street, unexpected but well received.

    Loved all the 2 hours of the interview and got some sick new music to listen to.

  • [music] [youtube] [bandcamp] チェスマスター : 非実体

    I don’t have the words to describe the experience of listening to this album, and even if I had I wouldn’t want to describe it.

    Futuristic, dark, unnerving, listen to this if you are looking for an auditory experience of those characteristics.
